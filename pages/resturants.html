Resturants

Someone once said that food was the way to a person's heart, and no where else in the world does this saying ring true than Asia! Japan is well-known for their strange and exotic dishes, as well as the hundreds of vending machines that offer anything from drinks to cupcakes to fruit. Below are a few suggestions.

Butagumi:

Between Shibuya and Minato City is a restaurant known for its pork dish, tonkatsu. It is a cut of pork that is coated in breading and deep fried to delicious golden-brown goodness. A Japanese dish that foreigners are destined to love!

Cafes:

There are several cafes scattered across Tokyo, Japan where an individual can order a drink and watch the world around them. What makes Japanese cafes interesting is many of them have a specialized theme, such as the famous Maid Cafes. There are also Cat Cafes and an Owl Cafe if you look hard enough!

Muginae:

A noodle restaurant, Muginae is found on the outskirts of Shinagawa City. Like Tamawarai, this place makes their Ramen completely from scratch and is extremely popular!

Otako:

At the heart of Tokyo, Japan is a restaurant that specializes in making Oden. Oden can be considered the equivalent of the American potluck, as it is usually comprised of a variety of meats and vegetables slowly simmered in a pot to create a warm, aromatic dish that is great for a cold winter day or night.

Tamawarai:

A Soba noodle restaurant near Shibuya City, this place offers homemade noodles that are so popular there is often a line outside the door! A must-try for any noodle lover that is in the area.
